Manufacturing Process

We utilize controlled rubber compression molding technology. Process includes:

  • Rubber granule processing
  • Density-controlled mixing
  • High-pressure compression molding
  • Dimensional accuracy checks
  • Strength and durability testing

Pollution Control

Our manufacturing process operates with controlled emissions and minimal waste discharge. No hazardous chemical discharge is released during production.

Quality Control

Each batch is tested for compression strength, density consistency, and surface finish before distribution.

Sustainability & Waste Management

Gomavision integrates recycled rubber materials into its production cycle, reducing landfill waste and extending material life cycles.

  • Recycling-based raw material sourcing
  • Waste reduction through compression reuse
  • Long product life reducing replacement waste
  • Energy-conscious production
