About GOMAVISION

CEO & Founder: Muhammed Esa Siyam

GOMAVISION focuses on recycling used rubber into high-performance rubber mats and flooring tiles. With the increasing global use of vulcanized rubber, waste control is becoming difficult. We provide a practical recycling solution by converting used rubber into valuable flooring products, for a pollution control manufacturing.

Our mission is to become a globally recognized brand in rubber mat and rubber flooring solutions while maintaining sustainability, durability, and affordability.
